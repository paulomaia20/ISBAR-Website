Identification


  • Full name, birth, gender and nationality of the patient
  • Name and function of the health professional
  • Service of origin / recipient
  • Name and function of the receiver health professional

Situation


  • Date and time of admission
  • Description of the current reason for the need for healthcare
  • Complementary diagnostic and therapeutic means performed or to be performed

Background


  • Clinical history
  • Dependency levels
  • Advance directives of will
  • Known allergies or absence
  • Relevant habits
  • Outpatient therapy and adherence to it
  • Invasive techniques performed
  • Presence or risk of colonization / infection associated with health care and measures to be implemented
  • Identification of the social situation and empowerment of the caregiver

Assessment


  • Active problems
  • Therapeutic drug and non-drug therapy instituted
  • Focuses of attention, diagnosis and active interventions
  • Significant changes in health status and evaluation of the effectiveness of measures implemented

Recommendation


  • Indication of continuity of care plan
  • Information about consultations
  • Identification of informal caregiver needs
